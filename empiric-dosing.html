Aminoglycoside Calculator
System Action Guidance (Applies to All Settings)
  • iVent: Open 'Communication to Clinical Specialist' for any aminoglycoside ordered for continuation >48 hours
  • Notify clinical specialist for follow-up on duration, monitoring, and need for pharmacy consult
  • One-time doses without clear continuation plan do not require maintenance dosing orders
Aminoglycoside Dosing Weight Selection
  • Primary dosing weight: Ideal Body Weight (IBW) for most patients
  • Obesity adjustment: If Actual Body Weight (ABW) ≥ 1.25 × IBW, use Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW)
  • Critically ill (non-obese): May consider ABW at clinician discretion
IBW Calculations
  • Male: 50 kg + 2.3 kg per inch over 60 inches
  • Female: 45 kg + 2.3 kg per inch over 60 inches
Adjusted Body Weight (when applicable)
  • AdjBW = IBW + 0.4 × (ABW − IBW)
Clinical Interpretation Guide
  • If ABW < 1.25 × IBW → use IBW
  • If ABW ≥ 1.25 × IBW → use AdjBW
  • If ICU patient and not obese → consider ABW with clinical judgment

Gentamicin/Tobramycin (Non-synergy – High-dose Extended-Interval)

Initial Dose:
  • 7 mg/kg IV x1
Maintenance Interval:
  • CrCl ≥60 mL/min: Every 24 hours
  • CrCl 40–59 mL/min: Every 36 hours
  • CrCl 20–39 mL/min: Every 48 hours
  • CrCl <20 mL/min: Placeholder dosing

Amikacin (High-dose Extended-Interval)

Initial Dose:
  • 15 mg/kg IV x1
Maintenance Interval:
  • CrCl ≥60 mL/min: Every 24 hours
  • CrCl 40–59 mL/min: Every 36 hours
  • CrCl 20–39 mL/min: Every 48 hours
  • CrCl <20 mL/min: Placeholder dosing

Key Principles

  • Dosing depends on effluent rate and clearance
  • Extended-interval dosing preferred
  • Therapeutic drug monitoring essential

Gentamicin / Tobramycin

  • Loading: 7 mg/kg IV x1
  • Maintenance: Placeholder dosing
  • Redose based on levels

Amikacin

  • Loading: 15 mg/kg IV x1
  • Maintenance: Placeholder dosing

Key Considerations in Intermittent Hemodialysis

  • Significant drug removal during dialysis
  • Prefer post-dialysis dosing
  • Require serum concentrations

Gentamicin / Tobramycin

  • Initial: 3 mg/kg IV x1
  • Maintenance: Placeholder dosing
  • Redose based on HD schedule

Amikacin

  • Initial: 10 mg/kg IV x1
  • Maintenance: Placeholder dosing

Gentamicin (Gram-Positive Synergy – Endocarditis)

  • Indication: Gram-positive synergy (e.g., enterococcal or staphylococcal endocarditis)
Dose
  • 1 mg/kg IV every 8 hours
Maintenance Interval (renal adjustment)
  • CrCl ≥60 mL/min: Every 8 hours
  • CrCl 40–59 mL/min: Every 12 hours
  • CrCl 20–39 mL/min: Every 24 hours
  • CrCl <20 mL/min: Give initial dose, then placeholder dosing
