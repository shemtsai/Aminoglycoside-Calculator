Parameter Estimation from Two Levels
Elimination Rate Constant (ke)
ke = ln(C₁ / C₂)t₂ − t₁
t₁, t₂ = hours after start of infusion
Elimination Half-Life (t½)
t½ = 0.693ke
Extrapolated Peak (Cmax)
Cmax = C₁e−ke(t₁ − t′)
t′ = infusion duration (hr)
Extrapolated Trough (Cmin)
Cmin = Cmax · e−ke(τ − t′)
τ = dosing interval (hr)
Volume of Distribution (Vd)
Vd = (Dose/t′) · (1 − e−ket′) Cmax · ke · (1 − e−keτ)
Clearance (Clr)
Clr = Vd · ke
L/hr — multiply by 16.67 for mL/min
AUC₂₄
AUC24 = Doseke · Vd · 24τ
mg·h/L
